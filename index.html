Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05 June 2025
Last Updated: 05 June 2025

HydroPulse is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Location Data

We request access to your approximate or precise location (with your permission) to recommend daily water intake based on local weather conditions.

Your location data is used solely on your device and never stored or shared.

b) Device Information

Basic device information such as model, OS version, and language settings may be accessed temporarily to optimize functionality. However, no data is collected or transmitted to us or any third party.

2. Permissions We Request

a) Location Permission

Used only to personalize hydration goals based on your weather. Not stored or shared.

b) Notification Permission

Allows us to send helpful reminders to drink water. You can disable this in your device settings anytime.

3. Ads and Third-Party Services

HydroPulse uses the TopOn Ads SDK to serve non-personalized in-app ads. No personal or sensitive data is collected or shared. Ad identifiers like Advertising ID may be used strictly for anonymous ad serving within the app environment.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All hydration tracking and preferences are stored only on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

5. Your Rights

You are in full control of your data:

  • Revoke location and notification permissions via device settings
  • Manage ad preferences using your device’s privacy options

6. Children’s Privacy

HydroPulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children or store any identifiable information.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Any significant changes will be communicated via in-app notices or updates.
